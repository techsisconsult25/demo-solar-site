Our Story

Why We Started Jimoh Solar

Jimoh Solar started with a simple frustration: too many Nigerian homes and businesses were stuck choosing between expensive, unreliable generators and solar systems that were oversold, undersized, or abandoned the moment the installer got paid.

We set out to do it differently — real site assessments instead of guesswork, transparent Naira pricing instead of forex surprises, and a local team that's still here long after your panels go up. Today, we've helped hundreds of homes and businesses across Nigeria cut their dependence on fuel and finally get power they can count on.
